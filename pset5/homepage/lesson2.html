LESSON 2

Key phrase

これは何ですか - kore wa nan des ka - what is this?

Vocabulary

はい - hai - for drawing attention

どうぞ - dōso - please

これ - kore - this (closer to the speaker)

それ - sore - that (closer to the listener)

あれ - are - that one (far from both the speaker and the listener)

お土産 - omiyage - souvenir

ありがとうございます - arigatō gosaimas - thank you very much

どういたしまして - dōitashimashite - you are welcome

何時ですか - nanji des ka - what time in it?

何人ですか - nannin des ka - how many people are there?

Sound words

すたすた - sta-sta - walking quickly

のるのる - noru-noru - walking slowly *it's vexing* or something like cars in a traffic jam

Grammar tips

の- no - a particle linking two nouns, possession

Tokyo no omiyage - souvenirs from Tokyo
